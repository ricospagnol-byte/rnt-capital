Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data. Written in plain English.

Last updated: 8 May 2026 · Effective immediately

1. Who we are

RNT Capital ("RNT", "we", "us", "our") is a trading education and signals community based in Malta. We are the data controller for the personal data collected through this website (rntcapital).

You can reach us at rntcapital365@gmail.com for any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights described below.

2. What data we collect

We only collect data you give us directly. We do not run any analytics, advertising, or tracking pixels. The categories of personal data we hold are:

  • Membership application data. When you fill out our application form: your name, email address, trading experience level, the markets you trade, and a short free-text answer about why you want to join.
  • Email correspondence. If you email us at rntcapital365@gmail.com, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.
  • Cookie / consent preferences. Your cookie choice is stored locally in your browser (in localStorage) so we can remember it. This never reaches our servers.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. Our service is for adults only.

3. Why we collect it (legal basis)

Under Article 6 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)). For processing your application data and for any optional cookie categories. You give consent by ticking the boxes on the application form and accepting cookies.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)). For replying to direct emails you send us and for the basic operation of the website. Our legitimate interest is running and communicating about our service. You can object to this processing at any time.

We use your data to assess your application, get back to you, and (if you join) to communicate about the community. We do not use it for marketing to third parties or sell it to anyone. Ever.

4. How we store and protect your data

Application form submissions are sent through Web3Forms, a form-handling service, and delivered to our inbox at Gmail (Google Workspace). From there your data lives in our email inbox until we no longer need it (see retention below).

We use industry-standard security measures. Including transport-layer encryption (HTTPS) for all data in transit, and the access controls provided by our email provider. Access to applications is limited to the two co-founders.

5. Third-party services we use

We rely on a small number of third parties to run the site. Each one is listed below with what data they receive and why.

  • GitHub Pages (web hosting). Serves the website. Receives standard server-log data such as your IP address. GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Web3Forms (form processing). Receives the contents of your application when you submit the form, and forwards it to us by email. Web3Forms privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts (typography). Serves the Inter font used on the site. May receive your IP address. Google's privacy policy.
  • TradingView (price chart, optional). Only loaded if you enable "functional" cookies. Provides a live EUR/USD price chart on the homepage and may set its own cookies on tradingview.com. TradingView's privacy policy.
  • Google / Gmail (email). We read and reply to your application from a Gmail inbox. Google's privacy policy.
  • Instagram (Meta). We link to our public Instagram page. We do not embed any Instagram trackers on this site, but visiting our profile is governed by Meta's privacy policy.

6. International data transfers

Some of the providers above (GitHub, Google, Web3Forms, TradingView, Meta) are based outside the European Economic Area, primarily in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the EEA we rely on the safeguards each provider has in place, typically the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses, to ensure your data is protected to a standard equivalent to the GDPR.

7. Your rights under the GDPR

If you're in the EU, EEA, UK, or Malta, you have the following rights over your personal data. To exercise any of them, email us at rntcapital365@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days and will not charge you for it.

  • Right of access. You can ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification. You can ask us to correct anything that's wrong or incomplete.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"). You can ask us to delete your data when it's no longer needed for the purpose it was collected.
  • Right to restriction. You can ask us to stop using your data while we sort out a query.
  • Right to data portability. You can ask for your data in a portable format (we'll send it as JSON or CSV).
  • Right to object. You can object to processing based on our legitimate interests at any time.
  • Right to withdraw consent. Where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it any time. This won't affect the lawfulness of any processing we did before you withdrew it.

8. Cookies

We do not set any tracking cookies of our own. The only cookie-like storage we use is a single localStorage entry to remember your cookie preference between visits. This is strictly necessary and does not require consent.

If you accept "functional" cookies, a TradingView price chart loads on the homepage. TradingView may then set its own cookies on tradingview.com. See their privacy policy for details. You can change your choice any time using the Cookie preferences link in the site footer.

9. How long we keep your data

  • Application form submissions. Kept for up to 24 months from the date you applied, so we can respond, follow up, and re-evaluate if circumstances change. After that the email is deleted.
  • Email correspondence. Kept for as long as the conversation is active plus a reasonable archival period (up to 24 months), then deleted.
  • If you join the network. Your data is kept for the duration of your membership and up to 24 months after you leave.
  • Cookie preference. Stored in your browser until you clear it or change it.

You can ask us to delete your data sooner. See "Your rights" above.

10. How to make a privacy request

Email rntcapital365@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy request" and tell us what you'd like us to do (access, delete, correct, etc.). We may need to verify it's really you before acting on the request. Usually a reply from the same email address you originally used is enough.

11. Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data-protection supervisory authority. If you're in Malta, that's the Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC):

  • Website: idpc.org.mt
  • Email: idpc.info@idpc.org.mt
  • Address: Level 2, Airways House, High Street, Sliema SLM 1549, Malta

If you're outside Malta, you may also complain to your local supervisory authority.

12. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top and (for significant changes) post a notice on the site. Continued use of the service after the change means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy or how we handle your data? Email rntcapital365@gmail.com.
