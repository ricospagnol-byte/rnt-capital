About RNT Capital

Two traders. One simple idea —
show your work.

Our story

RNT started the way most good things do — over too much coffee and a shared frustration. We were tired of watching trading get sold like a magic trick: copy this signal, follow this guru, get rich by Friday.

So we built RNT — short for Rise · Network · Trade — as the version we wished existed when we started. We post our setups and signals, but we always show the chart, the levels, and the why. The trade is the easy part — the thinking behind it is what you actually need to learn.

We're not promising you'll get rich overnight. What we can promise is that you'll come out the other side a better trader — and you won't have done it alone.

What we're about

Three things we won't budge on.

RISE

Get a bit better each week.

Forget the home runs. We care about whether you're trading better than you did last month — and whether you can show why.

NETWORK

Help each other out.

No cheerleading, no ego. Just people who'll happily tell you when your trade and your journal don't line up.

TRADE

Show the working out.

No cherry-picked screenshots. When we post a setup, you see the chart, the levels, the risk, and what we did when it played out — wins and losses both.
