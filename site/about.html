About RNT Capital

Two traders. One simple idea: show your work.

Our story

We started RNT because we got tired of trading being sold like a magic trick. The traders we know who actually do well are the ones who put in real work, and most of them don't do it alone. So we built a place where that's the whole point.

RNT stands for Rise, Network, Trade. We post our setups and signals every week with the full reasoning behind each one. The trade is the easy part. The thinking is what you need to learn.

We're not promising you'll get rich. What we can promise is that you won't be doing this alone, and you'll come out a better trader for it.

What we're about

Three things we won't budge on.

RISE

Get a bit better each week.

Forget the home runs. We care about whether you're trading better than you did last month. And whether you can show why.

NETWORK

Help each other out.

No cheerleading, no ego. Just people who'll happily tell you when your trade and your journal don't line up.

TRADE

Show the working out.

No cherry-picked screenshots. When we post a setup, you see the chart, the levels, the risk, and what we did when it played out. Wins and losses both.
