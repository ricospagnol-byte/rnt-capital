About RNT Capital

One simple idea: show your work.

Our story

We started RNT because we got tired of trading being sold like a magic trick. Most traders who actually succeed learn from others and build networks. So we created RNT: a place to see real setups, learn together, and get guidance as you grow.

RNT stands for Rise, Network, Trade. We post our setups every week with the full reasoning behind each one. We guide members beyond the charts: on discipline, on building community, on turning knowledge into results. The thinking is what you need to learn. We're here to help you with all of it.

What we're about

Three things we won't budge on.

RISE

Get a bit better each week.

Forget the home runs. We care about whether you're trading better than you did last month. And whether you can show why.

NETWORK

The community grows together.

Members who help bring others in share in the upside. Nobody wins alone here. When the community grows, members grow alongside it. We'd rather everyone win small together than one person win big alone.

TRADE

Show the working out.

No cherry-picked screenshots. When we post a setup, you see the chart, the levels, the risk, and what we did when it played out. Wins and losses both.

In practice

What "show your work" actually looks like.

Every week, members get the full breakdown of what we're watching and why. Chart, levels, risk, outcome. Nothing cherry-picked, nothing hidden after the fact.

How this works

RNT is free.

We teach trading, post our setups weekly with full reasoning, and guide members as they grow, both as traders and as they build their networks.

Some members join a trading platform to execute what they learn. We're here to support you every step of the way, whether you're just learning or actively trading and growing your network.

Why RNT?

Learning to trade alone is hard.

You need people who've been there, setups you can actually see, and a community that holds you accountable. That's RNT. We're building a place where people serious about learning can change their future. Together.
