Frequently asked

The honest answers.

Do you post signals?
Yes — signals are part of the group. But we don't just say "buy here." Every setup goes up with the chart, the levels, the risk, and why we're taking it. The point is for you to understand the trade, not just copy it.
Do I need to be experienced to join?
Not at all. We've got people who've never placed a live trade and people who've been at it for years. The frameworks scale with you — you'll get value either way.
What markets do you trade?
Mostly FX, indices, crypto, and the odd commodity. The way we read structure and risk works the same across all of them.
How much time does it take each week?
Plan for around 3 hours: Monday's market read, Wednesday's lesson, Friday's journal. You can do it in less, but you'll get more out of showing up to all three.
Are you guaranteeing I'll make money?
Absolutely not — and anyone who tells you that is lying. Trading is risky and most people lose. We can teach you to manage that risk and trade with a clear head, but we can't promise outcomes.
How do I join?
Fill out the form on our contact page or email us at rntcapital365@gmail.com. We read every one ourselves and reply within 48 hours.
