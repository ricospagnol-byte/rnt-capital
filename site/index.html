Frequently asked

The honest answers.

Do you post signals?
Yes. Signals are part of the group. But we don't just say "buy here." Every setup goes up with the chart, the levels, and why we're taking it. The point is for you to understand the trade. Not just copy it.
Do I need to be experienced to join?
Not at all. We've got people who have never placed a live trade and people who have been at it for years. The frameworks scale with you. You'll get value either way.
What markets do you trade?
Mostly FX, indices, and crypto. Some commodities too. The way we read structure and risk works the same across all of them.
How much time does it take each week?
Plan for about 3 hours a week. Monday's market read, Wednesday's lesson, Friday's journal. You can do less and still keep up. You'll get more from showing up to all three.
Are you guaranteeing I'll make money?
No. Anyone who tells you they can guarantee returns is lying. Trading is risky and most people lose money. We can teach you to manage that risk and trade with a clear head. We can't promise outcomes.
How do I join?
Fill out the form on our contact page or email us at rntcapital365@gmail.com. We read every one ourselves and reply within 48 hours.
